Here are the main organs of your body and what they do:

*   **Brain:** The body's **supercomputer** and **CEO**, telling everything what to do, how to feel, and how to move.
*   **Heart:** A tireless **pump** that pushes vital supplies and oxygen through tiny roads to every corner of your body.
*   **Lungs:** Your personal **ventilation system**, bringing in fresh air and letting stale air out with every breath.
*   **Liver:** The body's **chemical factory** and **recycling plant**, cleaning out toxins, processing nutrients, and making important building blocks.
*   **Kidneys:** A pair of **water purification plants** that filter your blood, removing waste and extra water to keep your body balanced.
*   **Stomach:** A powerful **mixing bowl** and **blender** that uses strong liquids to start breaking down your food.
*   **Small Intestine:** A long, winding **absorption highway** lined with tiny fingers that grab all the good nutrients from your food.
*   **Large Intestine:** A **water reclamation system** that squeezes out any remaining water from your food waste before sending the trash out.
*   **Pancreas:** A **master chef** that sends out special "food-digesting" sauces and "sugar-balancing" messengers to keep your body running smoothly.
*   **Skin:** Your body's **protective shield** and **outer suit**, keeping you safe, controlling your temperature, and letting you feel the world.
