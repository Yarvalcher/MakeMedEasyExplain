MHC class II molecules are a class of major histocompatibility complex molecules normally found only on professional antigen-presenting cells such as dendritic cells, mononuclear phagocytes, some endothelial cells, thymic epithelial cells, and B cells. These molecules are important in initiating antigen-specific immune responses.
