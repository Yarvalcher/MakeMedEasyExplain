Imagine your body is like a big, bustling city. If you looked at all the space in that city, about six out of every ten buildings, roads, and parks would actually be made of water. This watery share isn't perfectly the same for everyone; it can be a bit more or less depending on your age, whether you're male or female, and how much muscle or fat you have.
