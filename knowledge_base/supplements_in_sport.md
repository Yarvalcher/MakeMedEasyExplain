Imagine your muscles are like sturdy ropes, made of many tiny threads woven together. When you lift weights, you're strengthening those ropes.

Now, think of **collagen** as tiny, super-strong "reinforcing wires" woven right *into* the individual threads of your muscle ropes. Taking specific **collagen peptides** is like giving your body the perfect raw materials – like high-grade steel – to build and weave *more* of these strong internal wires.

This makes your muscle ropes tougher and more resilient from the inside out, especially after a good workout!
